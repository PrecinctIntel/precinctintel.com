📍 Ward / District Issue Pulse
KANE COUNTY CHRONICLE
Fabyan Parkway Jail Redevelopment — District 13 Epicenter
🔴 HOT — The former Fabyan Parkway jail site sits within Kane County Board District 9 and District 13's overlapping service area and has become the defining local issue in both districts' primary races. The county board must vote on a redevelopment plan. Incumbent Linder's existing position creates a clear contrast opportunity for Jimenez. Property tax implications of any development decision affect ~28,000 District 13 residents. This issue has legs through the March 17 primary. (Kane County Chronicle, 2026-02-25)
COUNTY BUDGET
Property Tax Levy — Board Vote Expected Q2 2026
📊 EMERGING — Kane County's FY2026 budget cycle includes a property tax levy discussion expected Q2. Both Deniz (R) and multiple D candidates have made property tax opposition central to their platforms. The board's current composition leans toward holding the levy flat, but infrastructure funding pressure from the county highway department may create a contested vote. Any candidate who can credibly claim credit for a tax hold will have a general election advantage. (Kane County Board FY2026 budget documents; candidate platforms)

Demographics
District 13 Overview — Geneva / St. Charles / Elburn Corridor
Kane County Board District 13 covers portions of Geneva, St. Charles, and Elburn — a predominantly suburban/exurban corridor. Median household income ~$92K (above Kane County average of $83K). Owner-occupied housing rate ~78%. Property tax is structurally the dominant voter issue. Registered voters: approximately 18,000. (U.S. Census ACS 2020; Kane County Clerk voter registration data)
