What Precinct Does

An intelligence operation.

Not a press clipping service. Every month, Precinct tracks candidates, endorsements, entities, and media coverage across your race — then finds the patterns, anomalies, and infrastructure gaps no single article reveals.

🔗
Cross-Source Connections

Patterns that only appear when you read everything at once — invisible in any single article.

📊
Month-Over-Month Deltas

Per-candidate deltas on endorsements, media, and finance — so you see the trajectory, not just the snapshot.

⚠️
Anomaly Detection

Flags unusual signals like domain squatting, institutional endorsements without matching infrastructure, and campaign activity that doesn't fit the public story.

✅
Every Claim Cited

Every insight links to its source. If we can't verify it, we call it a gap.

🏅
Coverage Grade

Every briefing is graded A–F. You always know how much we know — and how much we don't.

Sources include but are not limited to: IL SBE Campaign Disclosure, Shaw Local, Patch, Chicago Tribune, The Illinoize, county clerk records, and candidate websites. County board briefings add village board minutes, local Facebook groups, and NextDoor.

The Briefing

Hours of context, compressed into one briefing.

Multiple sections, scaled to your race type. Briefing depth matches available intelligence — we never pad.

Briefing Sections
  • 👁 What to Watch Cross-source patterns you couldn't find on your own.
  • 📍 Issue Pulse Which issues are heating up, cooling off, or being owned by which candidate.
  • 🏛 Board Dynamics Voting blocs, committee assignments, resolution history. (county board)
  • 👤 Candidate Activity This month's moves: endorsements, statements, infrastructure signals, social activity.
  • 📰 Media Mentions Coverage volume, outlet tier, and whether it's likely to move anything.
  • 💬 Community Pulse Local Facebook, NextDoor, and village minutes — when there's no press coverage. (county board)
  • 🌐 National Climate How national approval numbers and sentiment connect to your specific race type. (state legislative races)
  • 📊 District Deep Dive Rotating monthly: turnout history, prior margins, demographics, and why it matters this cycle.
  • ⏱ Countdown Days to key milestones. Early voting, filing deadlines, primary and general dates.
  • 📅 Events & Deadlines Candidate forums, filing deadlines, early vote windows — all sourced from official records.
  • 🔍 Intelligence Gaps What we don't know yet. Honest about limits. Gaps that fill in over time.
Our Policy
You're getting the data, not someone's interpretation of it.

Every source is cited. Every gap is labeled. We don't editorialize, frame, or filter intelligence around a candidate's interests. You get what we found — nothing added, nothing removed.

Every subscriber in the same race gets identical intelligence. We don't advise or strategize — that's a different business with different ethics. Your opponent could subscribe tomorrow and get the same briefing. The value is accuracy, consistency, and the hours you get back.
