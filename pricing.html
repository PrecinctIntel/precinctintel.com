Common Questions
Does my opponent get the same briefing?
Yes. Every subscriber in the same race receives identical base intelligence. Precinct is a neutral vendor — we don't provide different data to different candidates in the same race. This is a core product policy, not a disclaimer.
What races do you cover?
Illinois political races from local (school board, county board, city council, village trustees) through state legislative (state senate, state house). We are currently in the 2026 cycle covering the March primary and November general.
What if there's no data in my race?
Some local races are genuinely data-sparse. If a briefing for your district would be too thin to be useful, we'll tell you before charging you. Intelligence Gaps are documented honestly in every briefing.
How do I access my briefing?
Each briefing is delivered via email with a secure link to the briefing page. No app, no dashboard — just a link that works on any device.
Can I cancel?
Yes, anytime. No questions, no lock-in. Cancel through the link in any briefing email.
